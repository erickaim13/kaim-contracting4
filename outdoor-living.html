Professional Outdoor Living Spaces

Your backyard should be an extension of your home. Kaim Contracting LLC designs and builds custom outdoor living spaces that bring real comfort and style to your property. From pergolas and fire pits to full outdoor kitchens and lounge areas, we create spaces people actually gather in.

We handle the full build from design through final installation. Every project is custom made for your space and lifestyle. We work with a range of budgets and can phase larger projects over time so you start enjoying your outdoor space sooner.
