Pressure Washing

Restore your surfaces to like new with professional pressure washing.

Professional Pressure Washing

Years of dirt, algae, mold, and staining can make even a well maintained property look tired. Kaim Contracting LLC uses professional grade equipment and the right techniques for each surface to safely and thoroughly clean everything from your driveway to your siding without causing damage.

We adjust pressure settings, nozzle types, and cleaning solutions based on what we are cleaning. Concrete, pavers, vinyl siding, wood decking, and brick all require different approaches. Our experience means a deep clean without etched concrete or damaged surfaces.
