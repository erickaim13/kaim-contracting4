Trees, Shrubs and Plantings

Expert plant selection and installation to bring your landscape to life.

Professional Plantings

The right plants in the right places transform a landscape from ordinary to exceptional. Kaim Contracting LLC sources quality trees, shrubs, perennials, ornamental grasses, and seasonal plants and installs them properly so they establish well and thrive for years to come.

We take into account your soil type, sun exposure, water availability, and the look you want when recommending plants. Proper planting depth, soil amendment, watering, and mulching make the difference between plants that struggle and plants that flourish.
