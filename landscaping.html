Landscaping, Design & Plantings in Methuen, MA & Southern NH

Full-service landscaping in Methuen, Andover, Lawrence, Salem NH and surrounding towns — design, installation, seasonal cleanups & ongoing care.

Professional Landscaping, Design & Plantings

Our landscaping team handles everything from garden bed installation and lawn grading to full property makeovers. We bring creativity, horticultural knowledge, and a hardworking crew to every project to deliver results that genuinely transform how your property looks and feels.

We work with both residential and commercial clients across Greater Boston and Southern New Hampshire. Whether you need a seasonal cleanup, a brand new planting bed, or a complete landscape overhaul, every job gets the same level of care and attention to detail.

Services We Offer

New beds designed, shaped, edged, and planted to your specifications. We handle everything from soil preparation and amendment to final planting and cleanup.

Leveling and grading to improve drainage, eliminate low spots, and create a smooth, even lawn surface that sheds water properly and looks polished.

Spring and fall cleanups to remove debris, cut back perennials, clear leaves, and get your property looking sharp entering every new season.

Fresh mulch installed throughout all beds for a clean, finished appearance. Mulch retains moisture, suppresses weeds, and improves soil health over time with each season.

Crisp, clean edges defined along all beds, walkways, and hardscapes. Sharp edging ties the whole landscape together and gives a polished, professional look year round.

Complete property landscape overhauls from scratch. We assess, design, and install an entirely new landscape for properties in need of a full refresh from the ground up.

Plantings (Trees, Shrubs & Perennials)

The right plants in the right places transform a landscape from ordinary to exceptional. We source quality trees, shrubs, perennials, ornamental grasses, and seasonal plants and install them properly so they establish well and thrive for years.

Large canopy trees selected for your space to provide privacy, shade, and lasting curb appeal. Properly installed with the correct soil preparation, backfill, and staking for long term health.

Flowering and accent trees used as focal points throughout the landscape. Selected for year round visual interest and sized appropriately for your space at maturity.

Foundation plantings and privacy hedges installed to scale for your home. Chosen for appearance, long term performance, and appropriate mature size for each location.

Low maintenance flowering perennials selected for your growing zone that return each year and fill out over time with very little ongoing care required.

Textural grasses that add movement and visual interest to the landscape while requiring very little water or maintenance once established in the ground.

Spring bulbs, summer annuals, and fall mums to keep your property looking fresh and colorful throughout the entire growing season from early spring through late fall.

Landscape Design (Planning & Layout)

Every great project starts with a great plan. Our design service turns your ideas into a measured, actionable blueprint covering plant selection, hardscape placement, drainage, and a phased budget — so you can install all at once or phase the work over multiple seasons.

A thorough walkthrough and evaluation of your property covering existing features, drainage patterns, sun exposure, soil conditions, and areas of concern before any planning begins.

A detailed plan showing the placement of every element including beds, hardscapes, plants, and lighting so you can see the full vision before any work starts.

Expert plant recommendations chosen for your specific growing zone, sun and shade conditions, soil type, and how much maintenance you want to take on.

Thoughtful integration of patios, walls, walkways, and steps into the overall design so every hardscape element feels like a natural part of the finished space.

We design to fit your budget without cutting corners. Options are presented at multiple price points so you can decide what makes the most sense for you.
