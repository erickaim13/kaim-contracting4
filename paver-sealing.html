Paver Restoration

What We Offer

Complete Clean, Sand & Seal

A proper sealing job is a system, not a spray. Kaim Contracting LLC restores pavers in three stages: a deep clean that strips dirt, organic growth, and failing joint sand; fresh polymeric sand swept and compacted into every joint; and a professional-grade sealer that locks it all in and brings the color back. Done in the right order, in the right weather, with real cure time.

Every surface gets assessed first because a five-year-old patio in the shade needs different prep than a fifteen-year-old driveway that has been plowed and salted. We quote exactly what your pavers need and nothing they don't.

Services We Offer

Surface-cleaner pressure washing that strips dirt, mold, moss, and weeds from the pavers and flushes old, failing sand out of the joints so the new sand and sealer can do their job.

Fresh polymeric joint sand swept in, compacted, and topped off across every joint. Once activated and sealed, it hardens in place to block weeds, ants, and winter washout.

Professional-grade sealer applied to a clean, dry surface. Choose a natural matte finish that keeps the original look, or a wet look enhancer that deepens the color with a subtle sheen.

Targeted treatments for rust marks, white efflorescence haze, oil spots, and deep organic staining before sealing, so problems don't get locked under the sealer.

Sunken or heaved sections lifted, re-based, and releveled before the sand and seal, so the finished surface is flat, safe, and drains the way it should.

Sealer wears gradually with sun, traffic, and plowing. We recommend a re-seal every 2 to 3 years in New England and can put you on a reminder cycle so your pavers never fall behind.
