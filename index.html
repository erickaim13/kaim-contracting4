Pressure washing uses high-pressure water to blast dirt off hard surfaces like concrete driveways, brick patios, and pavers. Soft washing uses low-pressure water combined with biodegradable cleaning solutions to gently clean delicate surfaces like vinyl siding, painted wood, stucco, and asphalt roofs. Using high pressure on siding or a roof can force water behind panels, strip paint, tear roof granules, or damage trim. Most house exteriors should be soft washed, not pressure washed.
