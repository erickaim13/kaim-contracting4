Paver Installation

Beautiful, durable paver surfaces custom-built for your home or business.

Professional Paver Installation

Kaim Contracting LLC specializes in professional paver installation for walkways, driveways, patios, and pool surrounds. We work with concrete pavers, brick, and natural stone to create surfaces that are visually stunning and built to withstand New England weather for decades.

Our installation begins with proper excavation and a compacted gravel base ensuring your pavers will never sink, shift, or crack. We cut every piece precisely, use the right joint sand and sealers, and clean up completely when done.

Services We Offer

Custom patio designs in any shape or size, from simple rectangular layouts to curved freeform designs, using high-quality pavers in a range of styles and colors. We also install front entry and backyard walkways that improve curb appeal and connect spaces throughout your property.

Full paver restoration including releveling settled or uneven pavers, resanding joints with polymeric sand to lock pavers in place and prevent weeds, and professional sealing to protect against UV fading, water damage, and stains. Brings your existing patio or walkway back to like-new condition.

Full driveway paver installations and replacements built on a properly excavated and compacted base. We also install driveway aprons, the paver border that frames your driveway entrance and edges, adding curb appeal and a clean finished look to any asphalt or concrete driveway.

Slip resistant paver surfaces around pools designed for wet conditions. We select materials that stay cool underfoot and hold up to pool chemicals, water exposure, and freeze thaw cycles.
