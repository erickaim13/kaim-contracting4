Paver Installation

Beautiful durable paver surfaces custom built for your home or business.

Professional Paver Installation

Kaim Contracting LLC specializes in professional paver installation for walkways, driveways, patios, and pool surrounds. We work with concrete pavers, brick, and natural stone to create surfaces that are visually stunning and built to withstand New England weather for decades.

Our installation begins with proper excavation and a compacted gravel base ensuring your pavers will never sink, shift, or crack. We cut every piece precisely, use the right joint sand and sealers, and clean up completely when done.
