Hardscaping and Stonework

Retaining walls, steps, and stone features that define your outdoor space.

Professional Hardscaping

Hardscaping is the backbone of any great outdoor space. At Kaim Contracting LLC we design and build retaining walls, stone steps, raised beds, and decorative borders that add both structure and beauty to your property.

Our hardscape work is engineered to last. Every retaining wall is properly backfilled and drained. Every step is level and anchored. We do not cut corners on the parts you cannot see because that is what separates a ten year wall from one that lasts a lifetime.
