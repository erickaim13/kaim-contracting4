Hardscaping and Stonework

Retaining walls, steps, and stone features that define your outdoor space.

Professional Hardscaping

Hardscaping is the backbone of any great outdoor space. At Kaim Contracting LLC we design and build retaining walls, stone steps, raised beds, and decorative borders that add both structure and beauty to your property.

Our hardscape work is engineered to last. Every retaining wall is properly backfilled and drained. Every step is level and anchored. We do not cut corners on the parts you cannot see because that is what separates a ten year wall from one that lasts a lifetime.

Services We Offer

Structural walls built from stone, block, or timber to hold soil, prevent erosion, and create usable level areas in yards with slopes or grade changes.

Natural stone and paver steps installed on slopes to provide safe, attractive access between elevation changes throughout your landscape.

Custom raised planting beds built from stone, block, or timber. A great solution for vegetables, herbs, or ornamental gardens that need defined, elevated growing space.

Stone edging and borders installed around beds, lawns, and hardscape areas for a clean, finished look that keeps materials separated and looking intentional.

Low perimeter walls that double as seating in outdoor entertaining areas. Built to complement your patio and overall design while adding real functional value to the space.

Natural boulder installation used as focal points, erosion control features, or organic design elements that add scale, texture, and character to the landscape.
